Introduction

The Cozy Corner

Welcome to The Cozy Corner, a bookstore in the heart of Boston, Massachusetts. We are a quiet, comfy place to escape amidst the big city rush. On one hand, we are a traditional bookstore, but on the other hand, we offer quiet rooms to reserve for reading and studying equipped with desks and comfortable lounging furniture.

Target audience

The target market is young adults, aged 18-36, who enjoy reading books and want a quiet place to read. They are either in college or working so they also want a quiet place to study or do work.

Their tasks and goals with this website are to look through the list of books and gifts we offer, buy books and gifts online, reserve a time slot for the quiet rooms, and check our hours and location.

Reserve a Room

The cozy, quiet rooms are equipped with shelves of our bestselling books in each genre, a comfortable lounge area to read, a desk, and no distractions. Use to read, study, or work peacefully.

How to book: Select a day from the calendar and up to 3 one-hour time slots. See photos below of the rooms. After using your e-mail address to reserve the room, you will receive an email asking you to confirm your booking. Confirm the booking through email and you're set!
